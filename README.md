# solar-analysis
Commercial Solar Market Analysis
This project explores the growth of commercial and industrial solar adoption across the United States, with a focus on understanding where the market is expanding, what's driving it, and what it means for businesses evaluating clean energy procurement.
Built as part of my transition into climate tech and clean energy, this analysis pulls from publicly available federal and state datasets to surface patterns in capacity growth, installed costs, and policy impact over time.
Data sources
EIA Form 861, LBNL Tracking the Sun, and the DSIRE incentives database.
Tools
Power BI for data modeling and exploration, Tableau Public for interactive visualization.
Key questions explored
Which states are leading commercial solar adoption and why? How have installed costs changed over time across different market segments? Where is policy driving accelerated growth?
